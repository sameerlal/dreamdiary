Installation

1 Download Zip File above into your downloads folder.

2 Drag the two files (Installation.command and NewDream.command) onto your desktop.

3 Double click Installation.command. This will create a directory in your /user/Documents folder. You can delete this file after double clicking.

4 Double click NewDream.command each time you have a new dream you wish to add. Organization is done automatically

Viewing Dreams

Dreams are sorted by date. You can find all of your dreams in /user/Documents/dreamdiary. To view, open terminal and type:

cd ~/Documents/ && open dreamdiary
